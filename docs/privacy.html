Privacy Policy

2-value Image Editor — Last updated: April 2026

2-value is a local desktop image editor for macOS, Windows, and Linux. This policy describes what data is involved when you use the optional Pinterest integration.

What data the app collects

The app only processes data that you explicitly provide or authorise. No data is collected passively, and there is no telemetry, analytics, or crash reporting.

Pinterest OAuth access token

When you connect your Pinterest account the app receives an OAuth access token from Pinterest. This token is:

  • Stored locally on your device only, encrypted using your operating system's secure credential storage (Electron safeStorage — Keychain on macOS, Credential Manager on Windows, libsecret on Linux).
  • Never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party.
  • Only transmitted to Pinterest's own API endpoints (api.pinterest.com) to retrieve your boards and pins.

Pinterest Board and pin metadata

When you browse your boards, the app fetches board names, pin counts, pin titles, and thumbnail URLs from Pinterest on demand. This metadata is:

  • Held in memory only for the duration of the session.
  • Not written to disk, not cached, and not shared.
  • Discarded when you close the app or switch boards.

What the app does not do

  • Does not send any data to the app developer or any third-party service.
  • Does not write to Pinterest (no pins are created, edited, or deleted).
  • Does not include advertising, analytics, or tracking of any kind.
  • Does not require an account with the app developer.

Disconnecting Pinterest

You can disconnect your Pinterest account at any time using the Disconnect option in the Gallery panel. This immediately deletes the locally stored token. You can also revoke access directly from your Pinterest security settings.

Children's privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes materially, the updated date at the top of this page will reflect that. Continued use of the Pinterest integration after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised by opening an issue in the 2-value GitHub repository.
